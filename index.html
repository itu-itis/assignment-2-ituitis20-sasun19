HISTORY OF SCHOOL

The Federational Hıgh School is a 54 years old state school with which had former name Kurttepe Hıgh School. In 1924, the school had been opened with The President's accompany. At first , there was just a building undone which was being shared with another primary school. Because of structuring substances , the school garden was dangerous for students and dirty including dust.There were only twelfe teacher and 120 student .As the time passed ,buildings have been done and formed into only high school with huge area that could encompassed a full size football stadium.Each year using government budget and the teachers additional supports new things were added such as dining hall ,tennis court and years after indoor sports hall.

After some graduations some of students could get into such good universities that school get fifth line in the high school rankings in all the city. Like this , one after another successfull results fifteen years from then raised gradually untill today , to second place overall the country . Even though we had nothing, we have created a school that in each field our students gets medals due to their success. Having own indoor olympic swimming pool, own stadium contribute our students to enhance themselves their majors easily . In academic field our graduates' results show that our school is one of the best high schools in all over the country. Well educated and experienced teachers our first supports in this success.
